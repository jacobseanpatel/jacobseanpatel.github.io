History Bowl

The Redding United History Bowl League is a newly founded extension of the National History Bee and Bowl competition to specifically serve Northern California. The League's inaugural season during the 2018-2019 school year will showcase multiple Norcal teams competing through the already popular National History Bee and Bowl. Participating High Schools teams will field both Varsity and Junior Varsity teams, similar to that of a sports team.
